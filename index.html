As the CDM narrows the algorithmic reach gap, advertiser market dynamics shift and regional language content becomes commercially viable — reducing the economic coercion that currently pushes creators to abandon their mother tongue. The CDM is self-terminating: multiplier values decrease toward 1.0× as platform language AI improves.
